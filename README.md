# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard – Power BI

This project is a **Car Sales Dashboard built in Power BI** to analyze sales performance and business growth.
It helps users understand **total sales, profit, cars sold, top brands, monthly trends, and region-wise performance**.

## 📌 Dashboard Objectives

* Track total car sales performance
* Identify top-selling brands and models
* Analyze monthly and yearly sales trends
* Compare sales across regions/cities
* Find best-performing salespersons

## 📊 KPIs Used

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Cars Sold
* Average Sales
* Sales Growth %
* Monthly Revenue

## 📈 Insights

* Top selling car brands
* Best sales month
* Highest sales region
* Low-performing areas
* Customer buying trends

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Excel / SQL

## 📂 Files Included

## 🎯 Business Use Case

This dashboard helps management **monitor sales performance, improve strategy, and make data-driven decisions**.
